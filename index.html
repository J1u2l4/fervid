About Fervid Solar

Fervid Solar is one of Zambia’s rising leaders in renewable energy solutions. Our mission is simple — to provide clean, reliable, and affordable solar power for homes, farms, and businesses across the nation.

With high-quality installations, professional service, and technology built for African conditions, we are committed to lighting up communities and reducing dependence on unstable grid power.

Together, we can build a greener, brighter, and energy-independent Zambia.
